White Chicken Chilli

Description

Warm up your fall days with this super easy, delicious, and creamy one pot chilli. You literally just dump the ingredients into the crock pot and let it work it's magic all day. Come home to a creamy, warm, and comforting dinner that is ready whenever you are!

Ingredients

  • Rotisserie Chicken
  • 1 can of chicken broth
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can of corn, do not drain
  • 1 packet of ranch seasoning
  • seasonings of your choice, I like just salt and pepper.
  • 8oz of cream cheese

Directions

  1. Spray the crockpot with cooking spray
  2. Pour in the tomatoes, corn, black beans, chicken broth. Sprinkle the ranch packet and seasonings. Stir together to combine.
  3. Cook on HIGH for 2-4 hours, or low for 6-8.
  4. When you have about an hour left of cook time, cut your cream cheese into cubes and add it to the crockpot. Remove the chicken from the bones, and shred the meat. Once the meat is shredded, add it to the crockpot and allow it to simmer for the remainder of the time.
  5. Serve it by itself, or serve it over rice. Enjoy!
