Cinnamon Brown Sugar Coffee

Description

This quick and easy coffee will be a great way to spruce up your fall mornings.

Ingredients

  • 1 8oz keurig coffee pod of your choice
  • 1/4c oatmilk
  • 1tsp of cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar

Steps

  1. Brew 8oz of your favorite coffee.
  2. In a frother or a blender, whip your oatmilk until it is thicker in consistency and has a foam on top.
  3. Add the brown sugar to your coffee and stir until dissolved.
  4. Pour the oatmilk into your coffee.
  5. Sprinkle the cinnamon on top of the coffee.
